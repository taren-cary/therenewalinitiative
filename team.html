Meet The Team

The people behind The Renewal Initiative — driven by purpose, rooted in community.

Executive Director

Adrian Jones

"Real change starts at the local level — and Adrian is dedicated to being part of that change, working alongside neighbors, partners, and leaders to build something that lasts."

Adrian Jones is a devoted husband and proud father of four, deeply committed to family, community, and service. Guided throughout his life by a strong sense of responsibility to protect and uplift those around him, his firsthand concerns about the impact of drug use and crime on families and neighborhoods shaped his perspective — and fueled his drive to make a meaningful difference.

With over 30 years of experience spanning both the public and private sectors, Adrian has developed a broad and deep understanding of systems, leadership, and cross-sector collaboration. Today, he is channeling that experience into what matters most: investing his time and energy into strengthening the communities he serves. He is passionate about advancing community development, promoting health and wellness, and advocating for a safer, cleaner environment for future generations.

As Executive Director of The Renewal Initiative, Adrian works alongside neighbors, corporate business partners, and elected officials to realize the organization's vision — transforming underserved communities into safe, healthy, and economically vibrant environments where residents and businesses can truly thrive.
